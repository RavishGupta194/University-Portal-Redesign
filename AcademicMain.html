Section One

The Ramanujan Block at Chitkara University stands as a testament to modern education and infrastructure, fostering an environment conducive to academic excellence and innovation. Named after the legendary mathematician Srinivasa Ramanujan, this block epitomizes the spirit of intellectual growth and discovery. State-of-the-Art Facilities: Advanced Computer Labs: Equipped with the latest hardware and software, the computer labs provide students with hands-on experience in coding, development, and computational research. Digital Logic Labs: Designed for practical learning, the digital logic labs are equipped with advanced tools and kits to help students grasp the fundamentals of circuits and digital systems. Smart Classrooms with Projectors: Every classroom in the Ramanujan Block is a smart classroom, featuring high-resolution projectors to facilitate interactive and visually engaging lectures.

Section Three

The CV Raman Block at Chitkara University is named in honor of the Nobel Laureate and physicist Sir Chandrasekhara Venkata Raman, whose pioneering discoveries in light scattering have inspired generations of scientists. This block is a hub of innovation and scientific inquiry, designed to nurture young minds and prepare them for future challenges. Highlights of the CV Raman Block: Physics and Engineering Labs: The block houses well-equipped laboratories dedicated to physics experiments and engineering practices, offering hands-on experience to students. High-Tech Classrooms: Classrooms are designed to facilitate dynamic learning, featuring modern technology, including audio-visual aids and ergonomic seating. Collaboration Zones: Dedicated spaces for brainstorming and teamwork allow students to collaborate on projects, fostering creativity and problem-solving skills.
